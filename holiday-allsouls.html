All Souls’ Day (November 2)

All Souls’ Day, observed on November 2, is a day dedicated to remembering and praying for the souls of the faithful departed. In the Philippines, it forms part of Undás, when families visit cemeteries to honor and remember their loved ones who have passed away.

Traditions include lighting candles, offering flowers, and praying for the souls in purgatory. It is a time of reflection, remembrance, and family gathering.
