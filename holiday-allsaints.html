All Saints’ Day (November 1)

All Saints’ Day, locally known as Undás, is a special holiday observed every November 1 in the Philippines. It is a time for families to visit cemeteries, clean and decorate the graves of their loved ones, light candles, and offer prayers.

This tradition reflects the deep respect of Filipinos for their departed relatives and their strong belief in remembering and honoring the dead.
