Halloween (October 31)

Halloween is celebrated every October 31. Though not an official holiday in the Philippines, it has become popular in recent years with costume parties, trick-or-treat activities, and spooky decorations.

Halloween also marks the start of the traditional Filipino observance of Undás, leading into All Saints’ Day (Nov 1) and All Souls’ Day (Nov 2), when families remember and honor their departed loved ones.
